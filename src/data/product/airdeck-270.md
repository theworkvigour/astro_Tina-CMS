## A dinghy that disappears in a bag

The AirDeck 270 is for owners who need a tender that lives in a locker. It inflates in about ten minutes with the included pump, planes confidently with a 6 hp outboard, and packs back into a bag small enough for a hatchback.

### Hull and deck

- 1.0 mm PVC tube with 3 chambers
- High-pressure drop-stitch air deck — rigid under foot, soft when deflated
- Self-bailing when under way

### What's in the bag

- Boat, bag, pump, oars, repair kit
- 25 L fuel tank as optional extra

### Best for

- Yacht tenders (sub-10 m)
- Weekenders with a small car
- Anyone who has ever wrestled a heavy dinghy onto a beach
