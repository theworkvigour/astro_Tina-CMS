## A tender that punches above its weight

The RIB 330 is our most popular small boat — light enough to be lifted on davits by two people, stiff enough to plane with a 15 hp outboard, and stable enough to host four adults at anchor.

### Hull

- 3 mm marine-grade aluminium (5083) seam-welded
- 3 independent chambers for redundancy
- Self-bailing deck

### Tube

- 1.2 mm PVC, UV- and abrasion-resistant
- Replaceable in our workshop
- Six grab lines and four lifting points

### Outboard

- Recommended 15 hp four-stroke
- Max 25 hp (for heavier payloads)
- Pre-rigged with battery, controls and fuel line

### What's in the box

- Hull, tube, console
- 25 L fuel tank
- Bilge pump, mooring lines, fenders
- Repair kit
