## Built for the daily grind

The 450 Patrol is the boat we would build for ourselves if we ran a dive operation. Reinforced hull, Hypalon tube, custom console with space for radios, sounder, plotter and EPRIB.

### Hull

- 4 mm marine-grade aluminium (5083) with reinforcement stringers
- 5 independent chambers
- Self-bailing deck with non-skid pattern

### Tube

- 1.5 mm Hypalon — premium UV and abrasion resistance
- Replaceable in sections
- 8 grab lines, 6 lifting points, 2 bow cleats

### Console

- Customisable electronics panel
- Optional T-top with lights and radar mount
- Storage for 100 Ah battery and 25 L fuel tank
