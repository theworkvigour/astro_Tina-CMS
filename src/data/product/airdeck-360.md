## The family weekender

The AirDeck 360 is the boat we recommend to most families. Long enough for four adults and a days-worth of kit, light enough to launch off a slipway between two people, and stable enough for kids to clamber around at anchor.

### Hull and deck

- 1.2 mm PVC tube with 3 chambers
- High-pressure drop-stitch air deck
- Self-bailing, optional bow locker for mooring kit

### Seating

- Two removable aluminium bench seats
- Optional jockey console

### Outboard

- Recommended 15 hp four-stroke
- Max 25 hp
